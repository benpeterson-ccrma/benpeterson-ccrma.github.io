Website Intent

My name is Benjamin Peterson (he/him). I designed this website specifically for my application to the Music Technology PhD/Graduate program at CCRMA. Its purpose is to highlight projects and personal attributes that I believe are particularly relevant to the program. This collection features detailed demonstrations and descriptions of projects briefly mentioned in my personal statement or cited by my recommenders. Through this organized presentation, I aim to showcase the value I bring as a prospective student. This format also allows me to share the more technical and auditory aspects of my work. As an additional feature, I've included an audio player widget at the bottom of your screen. This player showcases unreleased original computer music compositions, demos, and remixes. It is equipped with controls for play, pause, track selection, volume adjustment, and includes descriptions for each track.

Who I am

  • Backend Software Engineer

    With over seven years of professional experience as a Backend Software Engineer, I have honed my coding skills in the tech industry, including at the publicly traded Silicon Valley company, BILL.

  • Sound Designer

    I utilize software and hardware synthesizers, Max MSP, and all commercial DAWs in my quest to create and manipulate sounds, resulting in moving auditory experiences.

  • Tinkerer

    Driven by insatiable curiosity, I immerse myself in the intricacies of computers and music technology, constantly exploring and understanding their capabilities. This passion fuels not only my hobbies but also sharpens my professional expertise in technology and sound.

  • Artist

    Music resonates with me deeply, shaping my perspective of the world and my place within it. The profound artistic power of sound compels me to craft soundscapes that convey these emotional experiences, aiming to evoke similar feelings in others.
